About Resume Builder

Resume Builder is a user-friendly application designed to help you create professional resumes quickly and easily. Whether you're a job seeker looking to craft a standout resume or a professional in need of a polished document, our tool provides an intuitive interface to input your details and generate a well-formatted resume.

Key Features

  • Simple and interactive form for easy data entry
  • Ability to upload and include a profile picture
  • Dynamic sections for work experience, education, and skills
  • Preview your resume before finalizing
  • Save and retrieve your resume data locally
